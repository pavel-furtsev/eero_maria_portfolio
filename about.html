About me

Hello there!
My name is Maria Eero, I'm a young upcoming architect-designer and a creative seeker from Estonia. In 2016 I received my bachelor degree in architecture at Nizhny Novgorod State University (Russia). Being a student I got that architecture is a connecting link in the regulation and governing the quality of life. I believe that architecture can make a difference and I aspire to be a part of that change. In September I was accepted for an Erasmus EVS project in Austria. I choose work with disables people, I wanted to see and understand with my own eyes their position in society and how with an architectural design we can make their life easier and comfortable. I have a dream to work with an architecture team on creating urban spaces in cities which would support the needs of disables people, a place of safety, that would free and inspire them. I hope to develop in the sphere of ecological architecture and green technologies that needed to design environmentally friendly and sustainable buildings. From the fact that in life I moved a lot to different countries, I learned to easily adapt and find contact with new people. I want to work, where I have not been yet, in a different country with different culture. I am passionate, self-driven, hardworking and willing to go above and beyond to design spaces or objects that best fit our lifestyle.
